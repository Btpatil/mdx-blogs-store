# How to Enable Horizontal Recent Apps in MIUI?

The horizontal recent apps feature in MIUI allows you to view your recent apps in a horizontal scrolling list. This gives your device a whole new look and feel, as if you are using a pixel device! If you don't like the look of recents page or you are simply bored of that vertical recent page, then you are on the right place. 

### To enable the horizontal recent apps feature, follow these steps:

1. Open the Settings app.
2. Tap on Home screen.
3. Scroll down and tap on Arrange items in recents.
4. Select Horizontal.

Once you have enabled the horizontal recent apps feature, you can view your recent apps by swiping up from the bottom of the screen. The recent apps will be displayed in a horizontal scrolling list.

### Here are some tips on how to use the horizontal recent apps feature:

1. To close an app, swipe left or right on the app's thumbnail.
2. To open an app, tap on its thumbnail.
3. To pin an app to the top of the recent apps list, long-press on the app's thumbnail and then tap on Pin.
4. To clear all recent apps, swipe up from the bottom of the screen and then tap on Clear all.
